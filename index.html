Welcome to the Recourses API!


This is an API of course review data collected over the academic year 2021-22 on courses offered at Ashoka university.

You can access this data across three views:


  • Access data by individual course at /course. You can filter by course code, name, faculty, semester, department, or its average ratings (greater than and less than).
    For example, here is every FC offered in the Spring 2022 semester which received an average holistic rating less than 3: ./api/course?department=FC&semester=Spring%202022&holistic=-3


  • Access data by individual professor at /prof. You can filter by name, email, qualification, position, department, courses offered or average ratings (greater than and less than).
    Here is every professor in the CS department who has a PhD and received an average engaging rating greater than 2: ./api/prof?department=CS&qualification=ph.d&engaging=2


  • Access data by individual review at /review. You can filter by course code, semester, verified status, words in the review, or its ratings (greater than and less than).
    Here is every review on an Economics course in the Spring 2022 semester which has the word "tough" in it: ./api/review?code=ECO&semester=Spring 2022&review=tough
